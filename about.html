Hello, I'm Jordan.

Outside of work, my days are filled with barking dogs, smoke-filled kitchens, crying babies, and the comforting aroma of fresh coffee. A little chaos, a lot of joy. I love watching my newborn daughter smile, taking my dog Rosie for long walks, and discovering new foods, beers, and coffee spots. (I will happily drive more than an hour for good food)

Baking experiments, Costco runs, road trips, fishing trips, kickboxing sessions, and blasting hip hop or heavy metal the whole way; this is what makes life fun for me.

I bring that same energy and curiosity into my work as an engineer. I build things that are fast, scalable, and genuinely useful, and I enjoy taking an idea from the whiteboard all the way to production. Being a full-stack developer allows me to solve complex problems creatively while creating experiences that make people's lives better. I try to live fully, with passion and curiosity, and I am always excited for the next chapter.
